About Us

A Legacy of Superior Bat Making

  • The company was founded in 1908 and was one of the primary bat suppliers to the 1908 World Series Champion Cubs
  • After the Cubs historic 1908 campaign and their superior offense, word spread about Kellogg Baseball Bats
  • The Pittsburgh Pirates became a huge buyer of these bats which have been outfitting every World Series Championship team since the 1908 Cubs

    • Today, the company is helmed by one of baseball's finest minds, Dean Cornelli
